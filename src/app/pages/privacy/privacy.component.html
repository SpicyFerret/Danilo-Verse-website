Privacy Policy

Welcome to my Privacy Policy. At neumannmarques.com, I value and respect your privacy, and I am committed to protecting your personal information.

When you visit neumannmarques.com, I collect the following information through the contact page:

This information is collected for the sole purpose of responding to your inquiries and providing the information you request.

The information collected is used exclusively for communication purposes. I do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties.

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact me at contact@neumannmarques.com.

8. Compliance

I am committed to complying with applicable data protection laws, including Lei Geral de Proteção de Dados (LGPD) in Brazil.

9. Legal Basis

The legal basis for processing personal data on neumannmarques.com is specified in Article 7 of the LGPD, including consent, contract execution, legal obligation, legitimate interest, and other lawful bases as applicable.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact me at contact@neumannmarques.com.
